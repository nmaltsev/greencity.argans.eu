About

ARGANS, is a fast-growing UK SME specialising in satellite remote sensing applications and services, delivering innovative solutions to satellite derived bathymetry, coastal charting and mapping, climate change monitoring, environmental information, and geospatial intelligence.

ARGANS, a member of the ACRI Group, is based in Plymouth, UK with offices in Sophia-Antipolis and Toulouse, France.
